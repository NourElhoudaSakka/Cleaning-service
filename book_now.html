Who are we?

TwoClean is a cleaning service startup launched in 2023. It is the innovative platform revolutionizing the world of household cleaning. At 2CLEAN, our mission is to simplify the search for cleaning employment opportunities and facilitate connections with individuals seeking reliable cleaning services. We came up with the idea of offering cleaning service by two persons who can be siblings, friends, etc. Thus, cleaning is less boring and takes less time Clients will also pay half of the price and get the work done in half of the time.
